Consciousness is, in my understanding, not a function or form*, but the direct appreciation of and participation in form-functions. I suggest that what we perceive of the public universe is only half of the universe; the half that has, for us, already been made explicit as an effect. In the jargon of Quantum Mechanics, the wavefunction has collapsed, or become decoherent.

The working assumption for QM collapse is superposition, in which all of the possible outcomes are present as probabilistic potentials, until measurement freezes the potential into the actual. Given that the letter psi is often used as notation for the wavefunction. Ψ, I propose that a sideways psi Œ can help illustrate the orthogonal relation which awareness (not human awareness, but awareness on the subatomic level as well) has to measurement. I call this symbol Qui' (who).
